## The Problem

India's 450 million unorganized workers face three daily crises:

| Problem | Scale | Status Quo |
|---|---|---|
| Wage theft & delays | 450M workers | No payment proof |
| SME invoice delays | ₹10.7L Cr locked | 60-90 day wait |
| Remittance fees | 6.5% per transfer | 2-3 day delivery |

---

## The Solution

KaamPay solves all three using Solana + USDC stablecoins:

**⚡ InstaPay** — Contractors pay workers daily wages in USDC instantly. Private via Umbra SDK. Permanent proof on Solana.

**📄 InvoiceChain** — SME invoices settled via smart escrow in seconds. Buyer deposits USDC, releases on delivery confirmation.

**🏠 HomeRemit** — Workers send money home at 0.1% fee (vs 6.5% bank fee). Arrives in 2 seconds, not 3 days.

**🤖 AI Assistant** — Hindi + English chatbot powered by Gemini. Workers ask "Mera balance kya hai?" and get instant answers.
